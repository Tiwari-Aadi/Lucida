How It Works
📚

Concept Parsing

AI splits your lesson into discrete concept chunks, identifies prerequisites, and maps the full dependency graph.

👥

Student Simulation

500 synthetic students across 5 personality types react to each chunk. See exactly where each type drops off.

📈

Dropout Prediction

Comprehension curves reveal risk per chunk with a 40% attention threshold. Get targeted suggestions.

🎓

Adaptive Reordering

AI suggests a better chunk order and generates bridge concepts to reduce dropout at critical transitions.

📊

Student Clustering

Groups students by failure patterns - early dropouts, mid-lesson drift, steady learners - with intervention tips.

✎

Differentiated Paths

High-risk chunks get 3 alternative versions: simplified, analogy-based, and example-rich, matched to personality types.
